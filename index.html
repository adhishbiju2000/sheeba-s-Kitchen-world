About Us

We specialize in a wide variety of pickles, Kerala snacks, and other delicious food items perfect for selling all over India. We also offer catering services for birthday parties, events, functions, and more, serving a flavorful assortment of Kerala meals that will leave everyone wanting more!



My Journey

From growing up in Kerala surrounded by the rich flavors of traditional dishes to moving to Mumbai, my journey to becoming a food creator has been filled with challenges and triumphs. I always had a deep love for cooking, especially Kerala's unique spices and cooking techniques. The dream of sharing my passion with the world led me to start this YouTube channel.

The road wasn't easy — there were hurdles like balancing work and passion, learning how to create engaging content, and overcoming self-doubt. But with each challenge, I learned, improved, and got closer to my goal of sharing the authentic taste of Kerala with everyone.

Today, I proudly serve a variety of traditional Kerala snacks, popular pickles, and much more. Along with my YouTube videos, I also cater for birthday parties, events, and celebrations, bringing the warmth and richness of Kerala meals to your special moments.
